Speaker Bio

Sid Patil is an Android Engineer and Kotlin Advocate based in Berlin, Germany. He currently works at Delivery Hero, building core ordering experiences for food delivery and Q-commerce brands including Foodpanda & Foodora. Previously he worked at Paytm & Insider.in, helping revamp Paytm’s payment flows. Prior to Paytm, built mobile learning apps for banks like Axis, YES and ICICI. He loves contributing to open-source software and building libraries to help others. Organizes local and global developer communities like Kotlin Mumbai and Android Worldwide.
