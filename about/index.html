About

42,023 views

Android Engineer and Kotlin Advocate based in Berlin, Germany. Currently works at Delivery Hero, building core ordering experiences for food delivery and Q-commerce brands including Foodpanda & Foodora.

Previously worked at Paytm & Insider.in, helping revamp Paytm’s payment flows. Prior to Paytm, built mobile learning apps for banks like Axis, YES and ICICI.

Loves contributing to open-source software and building libraries to help others. Organizes local and global developer communities like Kotlin Mumbai and Android Worldwide.
