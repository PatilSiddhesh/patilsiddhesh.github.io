I am Siddhesh Patil, an Android Engineer and Kotlin Advocate from Mumbai. I love building great applications and delivering rich user experiences. Currently I architect and build software for Paytm and Insider.in.

In my free time, I enjoy developing open-source projects and libraries to help others.
